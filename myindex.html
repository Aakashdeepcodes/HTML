Welcome to Vellore

Vellore, a city with a rich history and cultural significance, offers a blend of heritage, spirituality, and natural beauty. From the iconic Vellore Fort to the serene Golden Temple, there is much to explore.

Top Attractions in Vellore

  • Vellore Fort: A historical masterpiece with stunning architecture.
  • Golden Temple: A symbol of spirituality and breathtaking design.
  • Amirthi Zoo: Perfect for a day with family amidst nature.
  • Yelagiri Hills: A nearby hill station for peace and adventure.
  • Periyar Park: A tranquil escape within the city.

Fun Fact

The Vellore Fort has been a silent witness to key historical events, including the Sepoy Mutiny of 1806.
